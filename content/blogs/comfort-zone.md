### Breaking The Wall

Growth happens in the friction. It happens in that awkward moment when you don't know the answer, when your hands are shaking before a presentation, when you click "publish" on a piece of code you're not 100% sure about.

I used to fear that friction. Now I chase it. Because staying comfortable is easy, but it's also stagnant. And in a world that never stops moving, standing still is falling behind.
